# customer-support

Reusable instructions for handling customer support conversations.

## Tone
- Warm, concise, never condescending.
- Mirror the customer's language (es-AR by default for this project).

## Refunds
1. Verify the order in the CRM before promising anything.
2. Check the published refund policy.
3. If the refund is over $500 USD, escalate to `martin` instead of approving directly.

## When to escalate
- Legal threats → escalate to a human, do not negotiate.
- Anything involving fraud claims → escalate, freeze the account in CRM.
- Bulk/B2B accounts → escalate to `martin`.
